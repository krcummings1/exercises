California Dreaming

I recently took a road-trip down California's Pacific Coast Highway. I started in San Francisco and took 9 days to drive down the coast to San Diego, making many stops along the way. Here are just a few highlights from my incredible trip.

San Francisco

I spent 3 days and 2 nights in San Francisco. I went to the Golden Gate Bridge, Coit Tower, Lands End, Lucasfilm HQ, Fisherman's Wharf and many other amazing places. SF is awesome!

Written by Kaylee Cummings on January 8, 2015

Big Sur

I stayed one night in Big Sur at a creepy motel called Fernwood Resort in the middle of nowhere. I drove down the coast and stopped at any beautiful place I saw along the way, including Bixby Bridge, Santa Cruz, and Laguna Beach.

Written by Kaylee Cummings on January 8, 2015

Joshua Tree

Joshua Tree was incredible. I climbed massive rocks and was lucky enough to see both ends of a magical double rainbow on my drive in.

Written by Kaylee Cummings on January 8, 2015

Disneyland

Disneyland rocks! I met Darth Vader and Chewbacca and rode all sorts of badass rides. My friend Susanne and I spent 14 hours at Disney. We didn't leave until after 1am!

Written by Kaylee Cummings on January 8, 2015

San Diego

I only got to spend 2 nights in San Diego, which I'm still sad about. It was a beautiful city. I went to Point Loma, watched the sunset off of a cliff, walked around La Jolla and even went skydiving!

Written by Kaylee Cummings on January 8, 2015
